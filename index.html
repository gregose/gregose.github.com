Hacker Halted / 2009

So Long, and Thanks For All the Clock Cycles Awesome development and POC deployment of a distributed / crowd-sourced password cracker that gathers clock-cycles through XSS vulns. Projects

Rancilio Silvia PID mod

Work in progress PID modification to Silvia espresso machine to precisely control brew and steam temperature, monitor front panel switch status, brew group temperature, and make pretty graphs. Components include Opto22 SSR and SNAP-IAC5 module, Arduino and TC4 shield, and Omega Type-T thermocouples. Uses a RaspberryPI, Node.js, and D3.js for web-frontend.
